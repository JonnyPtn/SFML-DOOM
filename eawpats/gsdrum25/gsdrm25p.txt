GSDrm25p.ZIP                                          Oct. 24, 1994
------------

        This contains our GS Drum Set #25+.  Bank 25 is Roland's TR-808
drum machine.  Not only does this archive contain patches from that drum
machine, it also includes several patches from the TR-78 and TR-909
drum synth module.  That's where I get the plus.  =)  The GUS Patches
contained in this ZIP are as follows:
